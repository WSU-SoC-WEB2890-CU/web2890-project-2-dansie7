Inventor fueling a
Snowboard Evolution

Randall Wasserman, was one of those on the same slipery slope when trying to learn to snowboard himself. After observing the flaws associated with current snowboards that were not inherit with body movement, Randall set out to make learning to snowboard easier and not so complicated. In doing so, he invented & patented The Next Generation Snowboard which would take the snowboard world by storm.

Easy, fun, and simple. These were the qualities that the Randall knew that the next generation snowboard had to embrace in order to be successful. What does that look like? A retractable footbrake that allows a rider the freedom to decide when to slow down, and when you want to turn a revolutionary designed bottom makes it effortless. Short story, Storm Snowboards makes the sport of snowboarding fun not just for the few but by all!
