About Us

Do you ever see a skateboarder strap in before taking off down a ramp? Or a surfer attach their feet to their board before hitting that next wave? Here at Storm, we firmly believe that freedom isn't strapped in, it's being allowed to ride how you want when you want. This left us thinking, is there a better way to experience the slopes without the constant struggle of bindings? The answer is going to take snowboarding by Storm.

Next Generation Snowboard

Owning three design patents, start-up company Storm Snowboards has revolutionized the Next Generation of snowboards. Storm's design allows riders the freedom to ride however they want. You got it, ANYWAY YOU WANT! No more strapping in, simply ride the slopes as intended.

Never been on a snowboard, no problem! Storm Snowboards have been universally designed in a way that opens the door for all to learn to ride. Taking the pain out from learning to snowboard, and putting the joy back in. It doesn't get any simplier. The evolution of snowboards has arrived, take the mountain back with Storm.
